I'm Juan. I live in the Bay Area with my wife Rachael, our daughter Camila, and our newborn son Sebastian. By day I work at [Asana](https://asana.com) — the technical person embedded in the legal team, which mostly means I build tooling that makes lawyers' lives less painful.

I've been in crypto since 2013. What started as curiosity became a decade-long obsession with decentralized systems, DAOs, and the idea that software can change how people coordinate. I'm deep in the [Nouns DAO](https://nouns.wtf) ecosystem, active on [Farcaster](https://warpcast.com/gramajo.eth), and currently learning about zero-knowledge proofs.

---

## What I'm building

**0773H** — a small web3 media project. The name is a reference to the block height of the Ethereum genesis block's coinbase address. It's my corner of the internet for experimenting with onchain publishing.

**[nouns-builder-passport](https://github.com/jgramajo4/nouns-builder-passport)** — a project that launched via [nouns.camp](https://nouns.camp), exploring identity and participation in the Nouns ecosystem.

**[Nouns Milestone Payments](https://github.com/jgramajo4/Nouns_Milestone_Payments)** — smart contracts for milestone-based DAO contributor payments.

I'm also tinkering with Farcaster frames, onchain media, and tools that bridge the gap between "normie internet" and web3.
